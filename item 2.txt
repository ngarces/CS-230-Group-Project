Core Values: 
We want to be thought of as customer friendly, creative platform. 
One of our core values are Equality amongst our customers. We don't treat them as customers or people we are just working with, but as a collaborator. We respect ideas and creative vision, and we build and create something based on that. Whether you have an idea or not, we are always willing to establish some sort of idea for a project. We also don't favor one customer over the other.
