Ethics Policy:

1. Equality Amongst Everyone.
In our company, we believe that regardless of race, ethnicity, and background, all employees and customers will be treated equally. Having equality amongst the customers and employees will create a safe and comfortable space for everyone to share their visions in. At Graphic Gorillaz, Unequality will not be tolerated under any cirsumstances.

2. Respecting customer privacy.
  - As a company, our customer and their information is very important to us. We work hard to never violate their privacy and do  not share anything without their consent. We believe in having transparency with our customer and always keeping them informed on what is happening.
